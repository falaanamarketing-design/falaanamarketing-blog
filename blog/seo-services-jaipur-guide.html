What Is the Difference Between On‑Page and Off‑Page SEO?

Another common question:

"What is the difference between on‑page and off‑page SEO?"

Let's break it down simply.

On‑Page SEO Services

On-page SEO is everything you do inside your website:

  • Page titles, meta descriptions, headings (H1, H2, H3).
  • Content quality and structure.
  • Internal links and image alt text.
  • URL structure and schema if needed.

On‑page SEO services in India ensure your pages are search‑friendly and user‑friendly.

If your on‑page SEO is weak, your content won't rank well, even if you have great ideas.

Off‑Page SEO

Off‑page SEO is everything that happens outside your website but affects your ranking:

  • Backlinks (from other websites, blogs, local directories).
  • Brand mentions.
  • Social signals (indirectly, through engagement).
  • Google Business Profile and local citations.

Backlink authority and local SEO fall under off‑page work.

The best SEO agency in Jaipur or SEO services company in Jaipur balances both:

  • Strong on‑page SEO
  • Healthy off‑page SEO and local signals
